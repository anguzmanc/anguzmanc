<h3> 📖 &nbsp;About Me: </h3>

I'm **`Andrés Eduardo Guzmán Cuberos`** an Electronic engineer from Colombia, having hands-on experience with software tools such as MATLAB, Simulink, KiCad (PCB design), Arduino, LTSpice, Qucs, Verilog, VHDL, Python, and intermediate-level Excel, including data analysis capabilities. On the hardware side, I’ve worked with microcontroller-based systems and platforms including Arduino, SDR, FPGA, Raspberry Pi and ESP32.

My interests lie in innovative projects and emerging technologies such as Telecommunications, AI, Robotics, Cloud Computing and Big Data. But I am open to explore new I+D+i areas.
